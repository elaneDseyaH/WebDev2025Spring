What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites. They help websites remember things about you, like login status, preferences, and cart items.

There are two main types:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary and deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Stay on your device for a set time period.

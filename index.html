What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored by your web browser. They allow websites to "remember" information about your visit, such as login status or settings.

What Are Cookies Used For?

Cookies are used for session management, personalization (like dark mode or language settings), and analytics to improve site performance and usability.
